AUTHOR / ABOUT
==============
Edit about.txt to change the "About the author" box in the
"Make your own" popup (name, role, location, link, and bio).

Profile photo (the round "dp" slot):
  Drop an image in this folder named:
      dp.jpg   (or dp.jpeg / dp.png / dp.webp)
  If no image is present, the slot shows your initial instead.
